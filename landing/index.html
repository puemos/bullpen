Research you can actually trust.

A few things that make Crazylines different from pasting a prompt into a chat window.

01 · Evidence

Every claim has a source

When the AI quotes a number or makes a call, you see exactly where it came from. Click through to read the source yourself.

02 · Position

A clear take, not a dodge

Every report gives you a call — bullish, bearish, mixed, or neutral — and how confident it is. No "on one hand, on the other."

03 · Scenarios

Three scenarios, side by side

Every report shows a base case, an upside, and a downside. You see the range, not just one confident-sounding guess.

04 · Private

Stays on your computer

Your prompts, reports, and notes never leave your machine. No account, no cloud sync, no tracking.

The four calls

Bullish — points up Bearish — points down Mixed — it’s contested Neutral — not enough to call it

How it works

Three steps. Then you read.

Step 01

Ask a question

Plain English. “Compare NVDA to AMD,” or “Is the energy sector overbought?” Whatever you’d ask a friend who reads filings.

Step 02

Pick your AI

Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or anything else you already have set up. Crazylines finds it and hands it the research tools.

Step 03

Read the report

A thesis, the key numbers, the risks, three scenarios, and a clear call. Saved locally, so you can come back to it later.
